At the start, the user clicks on an Ad that is displayed on a Publisher's website. When the user clicks on the Ad, the request is sent to impact.com's tracking servers, which records a click for that Publisher before redirecting the user to the Advertiser's website. Once the user lands on the Advertiser's website, they navigate to the page where they can make a purchase. After the user completes the purchase, the impact.com Javascript on the Advertiser's website sends a request to impact.com's tracking servers to track the purchase event. Finally, the tracking servers link the click to the purchase in a single browser session, providing the Advertiser and the Publisher with the necessary data to measure the effectiveness of the Ad and optimize their campaigns.
